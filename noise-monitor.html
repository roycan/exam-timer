Usage Tips

Getting Started:
  • Click "Start Monitoring" and allow microphone access
  • Adjust thresholds based on your classroom needs
  • Use visual mode for silent monitoring
Best Practices:
  • Place your device in the center of the classroom
  • Test different sensitivity levels
  • Use consistent thresholds throughout the day
  • Review alert history to identify patterns

Note: This tool works best in quiet environments. Background noise may affect accuracy.
