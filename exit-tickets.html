How to Use Exit Tickets

What are Exit Tickets?

Exit tickets are quick reflection questions students answer at the end of class to help you gauge understanding and gather feedback.

How to Use:
  1. Select a category or get a random prompt
  2. Display the question to your class
  3. Students write their responses on paper or digitally
  4. Review responses to plan future lessons
Categories:
  • Reflection: Self-assessment and thinking about learning
  • Comprehension: Understanding of concepts
  • Application: How to use what was learned
  • Feedback: Course and teaching feedback

Tip: Use exit tickets consistently to build a routine and gather valuable insights!
