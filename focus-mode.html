Focus Mode

Focus Display

Focus Mode displays a full-screen message to get your students' attention. Perfect for important announcements, instructions, or when you need everyone to look up.

Keep messages short and clear for maximum impact

Tip: Press Escape or click the exit button to leave focus mode.

Quick Messages

Click any preset to use it instantly:

How to Use

  1. Type your message or select a preset
  2. Choose a background color
  3. Click "Enter Focus Mode"
  4. Your message will fill the entire screen
  5. Press Escape or click the exit button to return

Pro Tip: Use focus mode when you need all students to stop what they're doing and pay attention to you!
